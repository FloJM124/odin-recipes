Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp oats
  • pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mash banana, mix with egg, oats, and cinnamon until combined.
  2. Spoon small circles into a non-stick pan on low-medium heat.
  3. Cook 2 min per side until set and golden.

Why it's great: No flour or sugar needed, and it feels like dessert for breakfast.
