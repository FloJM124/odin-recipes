Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed veggies
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Cooked rice

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, toss in minced garlic for 20 sec.
  2. Add veggies and stir-fry 4-5 min until tender-crisp.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, stir, and serve over rice.

Why it's great: Uses whatever veg you have and it's ready in 10 min.
