Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 small tomato
  • 1 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of a tortilla, place it butter-side down in a pan.
  2. Sprinkle cheese and sliced tomato on top, cover with the second tortilla.
  3. Cook 2 min per side until golden and the cheese melts.

Why it's great: Crispy, melty, and you can swap in ham or chicken if you have it.
